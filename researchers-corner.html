Researcher's Corner

Software Tool 'SPA Analystics 1.0' for Establishing Seed Production Areas

Developed by:
M. Sivaram & Sujith K. Surendran, Forest Statistics Department, Kerala Forest Research Institute, Peechi, India

In forestry, quality seeds are propagated for large scale planting so that quality timber/wood is produced for the desired end use. The seed production areas are established to produce quality seeds from the best trees. This is achieved by selecting and converting the best plantations into Seed Production Areas (SPAs) identified among several potential candidate plantations. The chosen plantation is converted into SPA by culling the inferior trees from among the trees available in the plantation. The individual trees were scored for the traits such as height, clear bole height, girth at breast-height, straightness and roundness of the stem, health and crown characteristics of the trees following a scoring scheme and ranked. The area of chosen plantation to convert in to SPA may range from a few hectares to large number of hectares having thousands of trees. Therefore, scoring and ranking of trees based on a set of traits is computationally intensive.Especially, if this has to done by the field staff, lot of mistakes may creep in and consume lot of time. In order to set up SPAs, software 'SPA Analytics' helps to prepare a list of inferior trees to be culled by ranking the trees based on traits. In this context, computer software tool 'SPA Analytics' aids setting up SPAs. For more details visit http://www.teaknet.org/download/Final SPA Report.pdf

Computer programme for generating seed orchard designs

Download Seed Orchard Design

This programme is made in MS Excel. After downloading, double-click on the file icon and make some modifications in the settings before the programme is run.

If the file is opened using MS Excel 2003, do the following:

Click on ‘Tools’ from the upper Ribbon Menu. Then click on ‘Macro’. Under Macro, click on ‘Security’. Then set the security level to ‘Low’. Close down the Excel and reopen the Seed Orchard file again.

If the file is opened using MS Excel 2007, do the following:

Click on MS Office button on left most upper position. On the bottom of the right side, you will find the Excel option button. Click on that and then choose ‘Popular Category’ and enable ‘Developer’ Tab. Click the Developer Tab on the Upper Ribbon Menu, choose ‘Security’ and enable it. Close the Excel file and reopen the Seed Orchard file.

Two important things to be noted in the layout are we have sacrificed the double ring isolation and switched to single ring when it becomes impossible to fill a position meeting all the restrictions. However, this will be a stray case. Similarly, the equi-replicate restriction is also lifted to avoid blanks in the layout. You can verify the number of replications actually realized by clicking on COUNTS button. If not satisfied, generate another layout. Modify the input values according to your requirements as regards, the number of clones, number ramets and number of rows. You may also use the 'Zoom' key to get the display to any desired level.
